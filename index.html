I am currently living in Leuven, Belgium and am looking for Computer Science PhD position or software development opportunities.

In the past three and half years, I worked as a software engineer for Garmin Corporation, Taiwan. As a software project owner of automotive infotainment system development, my responsibility in this role involved software development (user interface design, reliability verification, integration with hardware devices and new software version release) and collaboration with people from different roles to deliver successful products.

I recieved the master degree from the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taiwan University, advised by Prof. Yi-Ping Hung.

My research interests include Interactive Multimedia System, Wearable technology and Machine Learning, especially their applications of human activity recognition and analysis. My previous researches were focused on using sensors on wearable devices to recognize gesture and develop multimedia interaction to help people's health. Also, I'm fascinated by all kinds of promising applications of Machine Learning.
